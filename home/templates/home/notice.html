1) The Employer Survey Form (Duly filled and signed by the concerned Employer) after completion of their industrial training. The students can download the Employer Survey Form from the Institute’s Website i.e. www.tnp.sliet.ac.in and click on “Employer Survey Form”.

2) The students may also have required to maintain their industrial training diary. The students can download the Industrial Training Diary as well as Instructions & Guidelines from the Institute’s Website i.e. www.tnp.sliet.ac.in and click on “Industrial Training Dairy.
